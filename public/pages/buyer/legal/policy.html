Buyer & Ticket Policy

Applies to all buyers and ticket holders using Roll Show

1. Overview

This Buyer & Ticket Policy explains how buyers and ticket holders are expected to use Roll Show. It applies to any account that purchases or holds tickets, passes, or access through the platform.

2. Buyer Accounts

  • You must provide accurate contact and payment information.
  • You are responsible for all purchases made under your account.
  • You must keep your login credentials secure.

3. Tickets and Access

  • Tickets grant access to specific shows, events, or experiences as described at the time of purchase.
  • Venues or hosts may have their own rules (age limits, dress codes, safety rules) that you must follow.
  • Roll Show is not responsible for conditions at physical venues, including safety, weather, or local restrictions.

4. Refunds and Cancellations

  • Refund and cancellation rules may vary by event and will be shown at or before checkout when applicable.
  • Some tickets may be non‑refundable or only refundable under specific conditions.
  • If an event is canceled or significantly changed, we will follow the refund or credit rules provided for that event.

5. Conduct at Events

  • You must follow venue rules, safety instructions, and local laws.
  • Harassment, violence, or disruptive behavior may result in removal from the event and potential account action.
  • Roll Show may receive reports from venues or hosts and act on them if necessary.

6. Prohibited Behavior

  • Reselling tickets in violation of local laws or event rules.
  • Using stolen or unauthorized payment methods.
  • Attempting chargebacks or disputes in bad faith.

7. Platform Role

  • Roll Show connects buyers with shows, hosts, and events but does not control every aspect of each event.
  • We may assist with support, but some issues may need to be resolved with the venue or host directly.

8. Moderation and Enforcement

  • Roll Show may limit or suspend buyer accounts that violate this policy or the Terms of Use.
  • Serious or repeated violations may result in permanent removal from the platform.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Buyer & Ticket Policy as the platform evolves. Continued use of a buyer account after changes means you accept the updated policy.
