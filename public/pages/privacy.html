Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Roll Show collects, uses, and protects your information. Payments are processed by a third‑party Ticketing Partner.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account information (name, email, phone, city, state)
  • Ticket wallet data (tickets, QR codes, verification stamps)
  • Skater uploads (videos, images, descriptions)
  • Device and analytics data
  • Payment method selection (not full card numbers)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to manage accounts, deliver tickets, prevent fraud, and improve the platform.

4. Sharing Information

We may share limited information with the Ticketing Partner, skaters for verification, service providers, or law enforcement when required.

5. Cookies and Tracking

Roll Show uses cookies for login sessions, ticket wallet access, analytics, and theme settings.

6. Data Security

We use encryption, secure communication, and restricted access to protect your data.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, updates, or deletion of your account by contacting us.

8. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the platform evolves.
