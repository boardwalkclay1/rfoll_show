A New Way to Work with Culture

Roll Show is the first platform that lets businesses work directly with skaters and indie artists in a structured, trackable way—without losing the authenticity.

Instead of guessing which influencer or ad buy might work, you can plug into a living network of skaters, artists, and real fans. You see who you’re working with, what you’re sponsoring, and how it lands.

  • Verified business profile: Present your brand clearly to skaters, artists, and buyers.
  • Campaign & event opportunities: Sponsor shows, sessions, and drops that actually match your audience.
  • Talent discovery: Find skaters and artists by style, location, and vibe—not just follower count.
  • Structured communication: Keep outreach, interest, and bookings inside one system.
  • Future-proof: The platform is built to support agreements, tracking, and reporting as it grows.

Roll Show is for brands that want to be in the scene, not just around it. If you care about long-term presence instead of one-off impressions, this is your lane.
