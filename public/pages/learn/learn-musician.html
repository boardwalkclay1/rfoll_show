Built for Indie Artists

Roll Show is the first platform where your music, your shows, and your collabs live in the same system as the skaters and businesses you actually want to work with.

As an indie artist, you’re not just uploading tracks into a void. You’re stepping into a network where skaters can skate to your sound, businesses can book you for events, and buyers can discover you through real shows—not just playlists.

  • Artist profile: Lock in your sound, visuals, and links in one clean page.
  • Show & session opportunities: Get booked for live events, skate shows, and brand activations.
  • Collabs with skaters: Pair your tracks with skaters and moments that match your energy.
  • Business connections: Work with brands that want real culture, not just stock music.
  • Future-ready: The system is built to support rights, splits, and tracking as it grows.

Roll Show is designed so you don’t have to choose between being “underground” and being organized. You can keep your identity and still plug into a serious, trackable ecosystem.
